Steps to make Vegetable Salad

  • Gather and prepare your choice of fresh vegetables and optional protein sources.
  • Wash and chop the vegetables into bite-sized pieces.
  • Combine the vegetables in a large mixing bowl.
  • Prepare the salad dressing and drizzle it over the vegetables.
  • Gently toss the salad to coat the vegetables with the dressing, then serve.
